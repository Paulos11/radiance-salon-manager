## 1. Introduction

Radiance Salon Manager is a comprehensive web-based application designed for beauty salons to manage appointments, services, and staff efficiently. This manual provides step-by-step instructions for all user types.

## 2. System Requirements

### For Users (Customers)
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- Email address for registration

### For Administrators
- All customer requirements plus:
- Admin credentials provided by system setup

## 3. Getting Started

### Accessing the System
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the application URL
3. You will see the home page with options to Login or Register

### Creating an Account (Customers)
1. Click on "Register" button in the navigation bar
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Full Name (required)
   - Email Address (required)
   - Password (minimum 6 characters, required)
   - Phone Number (required)
   - Date of Birth (optional)
   - Address (optional)
3. Click "Create Account"
4. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

### Logging In
1. Click on "Login" button
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"
4. You will be redirected to your dashboard

## 4. User Roles

The system supports three types of users:

1. **Customer** - Can browse services and book appointments
2. **Staff** - Can view their appointments and manage availability
3. **Administrator** - Can manage services, staff, and all appointments

---

## 5. Customer Guide

### 5.1 Browsing Services

1. Click on "Services" in the navigation menu
2. You can:
   - View all services
   - Filter by category (Hair, Skin, Nails, etc.)
   - See service details (duration, price, description)

### 5.2 Booking an Appointment

1. Browse services and select a service
2. Click "Book Now" button
3. You will be prompted to login if not already logged in
4. Select preferred staff member
5. Choose appointment date and time
6. Add any special notes (optional)
7. Click "Confirm Booking"
8. You will receive a confirmation message

### 5.3 Managing Appointments

#### Viewing Appointments
1. Go to "Appointments" from the navigation menu
2. You can filter by:
   - All appointments
   - Pending
   - Confirmed
   - Completed
   - Cancelled

#### Cancelling an Appointment
1. Navigate to "Appointments"
2. Find the appointment you want to cancel
3. Click "Cancel" button
4. Confirm the cancellation
5. The appointment status will be updated to "Cancelled"

### 5.4 Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- Total appointments count
- Pending appointments
- Completed appointments
- Upcoming appointments (next 3)
- Quick action buttons for common tasks

### 5.5 Profile Management

1. Click on "Profile" or your name in the navigation
2. You can update:
   - Full Name
   - Phone Number
   - Date of Birth
   - Address
3. Click "Save Changes" to update

#### Changing Password
1. Go to Profile settings
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password
5. Confirm new password
6. Click "Update Password"

## 6. Staff Guide

### 6.1 Viewing Appointments

1. Login with your staff credentials

1. Navigate to "Profile"
2. Update:

## 7. Administrator Guide

### 7.1 Service Management

#### Adding a New Service
1. Login as admin
2. Go to "Admin Panel" → "Services"
3. Click "Add New Service"
4. Fill in the form:
   - Service Name
   - Description
   - Category
   - Duration (in minutes)
   - Price
5. Click "Save"

#### Editing a Service
1. Go to "Admin Panel" → "Services"
2. Find the service to edit
3. Click "Edit" button
4. Update the fields
5. Click "Save Changes"

#### Deleting a Service
1. Go to "Admin Panel" → "Services"
2. Find the service to delete
3. Click "Delete" button
4. Confirm deletion

### 7.2 Staff Management

#### Adding Staff Member
1. Go to "Admin Panel" → "Staff"
2. Click "Add Staff Member"
3. Fill in the form:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Password
   - Phone
   - Specialization
   - Experience (years)
   - Bio
4. Click "Create Staff"

#### Assigning Services to Staff
1. Go to "Admin Panel" → "Staff"
2. Select a staff member
3. Click "Manage Services"
4. Select services to assign
5. Click "Save"

#### Editing Staff Information
1. Find the staff member
2. Click "Edit"
3. Update information
4. Save changes

### 7.3 Appointment Management

#### Viewing All Appointments
1. Go to "Admin Panel" → "Appointments"
2. Filter by:
   - Staff member
   - Customer
   - Status
   - Date range

#### Updating Appointment Status
1. Find the appointment
2. Click "Edit"
3. Update status to:
   - Confirmed
   - Completed
   - Cancelled
   - No-show
4. Save changes

### 7.4 Dashboard Analytics

The admin dashboard shows:
- Total appointments
- Pending approvals
- Completed services
- Staff performance
- Revenue statistics (if enabled)

## 8. Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Cannot Login
- **Solution 1**: Verify email and password are correct
- **Solution 2**: Check if Caps Lock is on
- **Solution 3**: Use "Forgot Password" to reset

#### Appointment Not Showing
- **Solution**: Refresh the page
- **Check**: Ensure you're logged in with correct account
- **Verify**: Check the filters applied

#### Cannot Book Appointment
- **Check 1**: Ensure you're logged in
- **Check 2**: Verify the time slot is available
- **Check 3**: Check if the service and staff are active

#### Page Not Loading
- **Solution 1**: Clear browser cache and cookies
- **Solution 2**: Try a different browser
- **Solution 3**: Check internet connection

#### Double Booking Error
- **Cause**: The selected time slot is already taken
- **Solution**: Choose a different time slot

## Glossary

- **Appointment**: A scheduled service booking
- **Service**: A beauty treatment offered by the salon
- **Staff**: Salon employee who provides services
- **Customer**: User who books appointments
- **Dashboard**: Main overview page after login
- **Category**: Service classification (Hair, Skin, Nails, etc.)
- **Status**: Current state of an appointment (Pending, Confirmed, etc.)
